My Story

Born in Mirpur, Dhaka in 1998, Abdullah Al Fahim grew up as an active and straightforward child, moving through eight different schools across Bangladesh during his early education. Despite constant changes, he excelled academically. His professional journey began with leadership roles at Farhanasbrainstation and the National University Skill Development Forum, where he rose to become Executive Director of Business Development and Head of Branding and Communication, respectively. After graduation in 2023, Fahim transitioned into his career, joining Channel i as a Broadcast Engineer (Transmission) in 2024.
